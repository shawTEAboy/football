What is Football?

Football is a sport played by two teams. Each team has 11 players. The goal is to kick the ball into the other team's net. It is the most popular sport in the world.

I started watching football when I was a kid and I have loved it ever since. My favorite team is Real Madrid.

A game lasts 90 minutes with two halves of 45 minutes each.

Basic Rules of Football

Here are some of the basic rules you need to know:

  • You cannot touch the ball with your hands (only the goalkeeper can)
  • If the ball goes out of bounds, the other team gets a throw-in
  • A yellow card is a warning and a red card means you are out of the game
  • The team with the most goals at the end wins
  • If the score is tied, the game can go to extra time or penalties

Famous Football Players

These are some of the best football players of all time:

  1. Lionel Messi - he won the World Cup with Argentina in 2022
  2. Cristiano Ronaldo - scored hundreds of goals in his career
  3. Pele - won three World Cups with Brazil
  4. Ronaldinho - known for amazing tricks and skills
  5. Marta - the best female footballer ever
